why we started bioworks

4 letters make up the entire biological code. As a Biology and Technology company, we aim to program biological cells to do the heavy lifting; for the advancement of mankind. This will be achieved by translating computer bits to RNA & DNA and finally functional proteins that can direct cells to perform desired functions

Driven by programmers and biologists, we are reinventing how biology and technology interact providing solutions to modern day problems such as biofuels, better industrial processes, sustainable therapeutics and cleaner environments.
